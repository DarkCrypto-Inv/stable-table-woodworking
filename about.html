THE SHOP
Working shop. Real standards.

Stable Table is a working woodworking and restoration shop based in Cheboygan, Michigan. I approach projects with a mix of traditional craft and modern systems thinking—precision planning, documented steps, and durable finishes built for real use. I’m also a U.S. Navy combat veteran and a member of the Sault Ste. Marie Tribe of Chippewa Indians.

The goal is simple: honest work that holds up over time—without gimmicks, without shortcuts.
