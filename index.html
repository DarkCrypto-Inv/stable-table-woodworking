How technology is integrated

Woodworking doesn’t need gimmicks— it needs clarity, accuracy, and consistency. Technology supports the process so the craft stays clean.

  • Precision measurement and layout planning
  • Documented workflow and photo-based progress updates
  • Digital design assistance for proportion and fit
  • Repeatable joinery and finishing standards
  • AI-assisted concept exploration when helpful
  • Clear scope, timeline, and expectations up front
Process

Intake → Precision Plan → Build/Restore → Delivery

  • Intake: photos + basics, goals, timeline
  • Precision plan: materials, structure, finish approach
  • Build/restore: staged work, clean execution
  • Delivery: pickup or delivery (within reason)
