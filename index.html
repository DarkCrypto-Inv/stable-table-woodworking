About Stable Table

Built on Heritage.
Run with Discipline.

Stable Table is rooted in third-generation woodworking and shaped by a standard of workmanship that treats quality as the baseline, not the upgrade.

That traditional craft is backed by a modern, organized workflow and a level of accountability forged through military service. The result is a shop that values precision, communication, and work that holds up over time.

Whether restoring a family piece, refinishing furniture with life left in it, or building something custom from the ground up, the mission stays the same: create work with integrity and make it worth owning for decades.

Custom Builds

Built Around Your Space, Your Piece, or Your Idea.

For customers looking for something larger, more personal, or completely one-of-a-kind, Stable Table also accepts custom build requests. These projects are quoted individually based on size, complexity, material, finish, and timeline.
